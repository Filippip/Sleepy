About the Problem

Teenagers today sleep far less than their bodies and minds need. Late-night scrolling, stress from school, and irregular routines slowly drain energy, focus, and overall wellbeing. Quality sleep fuels creativity, growth, mood, and performance — without it, everything feels harder.

Our mission is to help you understand how powerful sleep truly is, and inspire you to build healthier habits that make every day feel easier, brighter, and more balanced.
